Vice President, Finance

Second-Year Position

DUTIES & TASKS

  1. Coordinate and direct the financial planning, budgeting, and investment activities of the Club;
  2. Develop internal control policies, guidelines, and procedures for Club activities such as budget administration, cash and credit management, and accounting;
  3. Direct preparation of financial statements, business activity reports, financial position forecasts, annual budgets, and/or reports required by Congress;
  4. Advise the President on short-term and long-term financial objectives, policies, and actions;
  5. Analyze the financial details of past, present, and expected operations in order to identify development opportunities and areas where improvement is needed;
  6. Delegate authority for the receipt, disbursement, banking, protection, and custody of funds, securities, and financial instruments;
  7. Supervise others in performing financial reporting, accounting, billing, collections, and budgeting duties;
  8. Conduct or coordinate audits of Club accounts and financial transactions to ensure compliance with MSU requirements and statutes;
  9. Develop and maintain relationships with banking, insurance, and non-organizational accounting personnel in order to facilitate financial activities;
  10. Monitor and evaluate the performance of accounting and other financial staff; recommend and implement personnel actions such as promotions and dismissals.
  11. Provide direction and assistance to other organizational units regarding accounting and budgeting policies and procedures, and efficient control and utilization of financial resources;
  12. Receive and record requests for disbursements; authorize disbursements in accordance with policies and procedures.
